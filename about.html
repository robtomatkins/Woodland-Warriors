Our Walks

Woodland Warriors host volunteer led litter picking walks in woodland spaces and parks around London every Saturday. Our walks are all led by a 'walk lead' and we have routes for all abilites and fitness levels, register your interest via our signup form!

Our Mission

Woodland Warrios aim to combat littering and fly tipping in woodland areas and parks around London, as well as provide opportinites for people to come together socially and work towards a common goal

Our Warriors

Our warriors are volunteers from all walks of life, who are passionte about preserving and caring for natural green spaces in and around London, as well as enjoying the social aspect of meeting up for a walk with other nature lovers

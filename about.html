Our Walks

Woodland Warriors are a social initiative that host voluntary group litter picking walks in woodland spaces around London.

Our Mission

We provide opportunities for nature lovers to come together to help preserve our woodlands spaces in a positive, social setting.

Our Warriors

“Our volunteers strive to preserve and care for our surrounding natural green spaces through our community led litter picking walks. We’re a friendly bunch and all our welcome, just choose a walk and register your interest via our website contact form”

Our Values

We believe in the power of people working towards a common goal, and the positive impact working outside in green rural spaces can have on a person’s well-being and mental health.
